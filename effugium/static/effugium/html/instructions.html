INSTRUCTIONS


Welcome to Roomworld!

Here, you will play a set of games. There are 80 games in total and it should take you about 20 minutes to complete all of them.


To navigate through the games, press the arrow keys.

If you get stuck in a game, you have two options:

You can try to solve the game again. To do this, press r. This will restart the game.

You can also give up and start a new game. To do this, press n. This will start a new game.


Please make sure to give the game your full attention.
Find a quiet place with good internet connect and turn off your notifications. You are not timed with how long it takes you to complete each game.
